Energy + Data + Innovation

Solving the next decade's energy challenges will be enabled by software, data, and AI.  Grids must absorb volatile renewables, buildings must answer to rising electricity prices, and industrial operations must squeeze efficiency from every kilowatt-hour. We back the founders building the intelligence layer between energy supply and demand.

Energy

Data

AI

Software, applied.
